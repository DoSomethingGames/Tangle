Example for Zombie Blood Drive.

If you donate times a year for years you have the potential to save lives.

  • US Population = 318,900,000
  • Eligible blood donors = 121,182,000
  • Number who donate = 9,200,000
  • That's less than 8% of the eligible population, and less than 3% of the total population
  • Number of blood transfusions needed per year: 30,000,000
  • That means each eligable blood donor needs to donate at least three times a year to keep up with demand, but currently donors only average a donation of twice a year.
  • That means there are 11,600,000 blood transfusions that are not being performed.
  • That's almost 4% of the population who needs blood to survive, and who will not get it.
